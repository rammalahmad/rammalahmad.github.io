      Proposition 2.12.2 (Etingof–Gelaki–Nikshych–Ostrik): If M₁ ≃ Mod_C(A) and M₂ ≃ Mod_C(B) for algebras A, B ∈ C, then the category Fun_C(M₁, M₂) of right-exact module functors is equivalent to the category of A-B-bimodules via the functor sending a bimodule M to • ⊗_A M.
