    Proposition 1.45.5: For a transitive unital ℤ₊-ring A of finite rank with Frobenius–Perron data, (1) FPdim : A → ℂ is a ring homomorphism; (2) there is a unique (up to scaling) regular element R with XR = FPdim(X) R for all X, which after normalization has positive coefficients, satisfies FPdim(R) > 0 and RY = FPdim(Y) R; (3) FPdim is the unique nonzero character of A taking nonnegative values on the basis; and (4) if X has nonnegative coefficients then FPdim(X) is the largest nonnegative eigenvalue of the multiplication matrix N_X.
